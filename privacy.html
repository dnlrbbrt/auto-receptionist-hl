1. Introduction

Auto Receptionist HL ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services, including our SMS messaging program.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI automation services
  • Send you SMS messages you've opted into, including service updates, appointment reminders, and promotional offers

Consent: By opting into our SMS program, you consent to receive automated text messages from Auto Receptionist HL at the phone number you provide.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your consent

Important: We do not purchase, rent, or use affiliate lead lists. All contacts are obtained through direct opt-in consent.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you opt out of SMS messages, we will retain your phone number only to honor your opt-out request.

You have the right to:

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. These may include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell your browsing data to advertisers. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Age Restrictions

Our services, including our SMS messaging program, are only available to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use our services or provide any personal information.
